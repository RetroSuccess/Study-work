Welcome to Ricfield

Richfield is a private higher education institution in South Africa that offers a range of programs in IT, business, and management. Established in 1998, Richfield has expanded its campuses across the country, providing both online and in-person learning options. The institution focuses on equipping students with practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge to enhance employability.
