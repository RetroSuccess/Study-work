About Skillet

Skillet is an American Christian rock band formed in 1996 in Memphis, Tennessee. They are known for mixing symphonic rock, industrial, and alternative metal. Their dynamic live shows and inspirational lyrics have earned them a diverse fanbase worldwide.

Band Members

  • John Cooper – Lead vocals, bass
  • Korey Cooper – Guitar, keys, backing vocals
  • Jen Ledger – Drums, vocals
  • Seth Morrison – Lead guitar

Discography Highlights

  • Collide (2003)
  • Comatose (2006)
  • Awake (2009)
  • Rise (2013)
  • Unleashed (2016)
  • Dominion (2022)

Fun Facts

  • Skillet has sold over 12 million albums worldwide.
  • “Monster” has over 3 billion global audio streams.
  • They’ve performed in over 25 countries.
